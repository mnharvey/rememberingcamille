# Remembering Camille Noe Borgia
### February 2, 1992 – May 15, 2025

This is a memorial website created with love for Camille Noe Borgia and dedicated to her husband Chase, her son Luca, and everyone whose life she touched.

**Live site:** [mnharvey.github.io/rememberingcamille](https://mnharvey.github.io/rememberingcamille)

---

## Our Commitment

We will do everything we can to maintain this memorial site so that Camille's memory has a permanent home on the internet. Friends and family should feel confident that this page will remain available as a place to revisit her story, light a candle, and share memories for years to come.

---

## Features

- **Tribute** — A written tribute to Camille's life and the lives she touched
- **Photo Gallery** — A shared Google Photos album where friends and family can add their own photos and videos
- **Light a Candle** — A virtual candle lighting in her memory
- **Guestbook** — A permanent guestbook where visitors can leave messages for the family

---

## For the Family

If you need to update any information on this site — funeral details, a photo of Camille, or anything else — please reach out through the Facebook page or contact us directly.
